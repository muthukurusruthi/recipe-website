Fried Rice Recipe

This is my sister's secret fried rice recipe, cherished for its perfect blend of flavors and quick preparation. It's the ultimate comfort food, bringing warmth and joy to every meal. Enjoy the love in every bite!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small white onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 package frozen mixed vegetables, cooked according to package instructions
  • 4 cups refrigerated cooked rice
  • 3 eggs, scrambled
  • 2 tablespoon soy sauce (or more to taste)
  • 2 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 2 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare frozen vegetables according to directions.
  2. While the vegetables cook, scramble the eggs in a frying pan, making sure to break the egg up into small pieces.
  3. Heat olive oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion, garlic and ginger and cook for 5 minutes or until onion is cooked and translucent.
  4. Add the steamed vegetables, rice, scrambled eggs and soy sauce. (Also add oyster sauce here, if desired.) Continue to saute, stirring occasionally, for an additional 3 minutes. Stir in sesame oil and cook for one minute, then remove from heat. Serve immediately.
